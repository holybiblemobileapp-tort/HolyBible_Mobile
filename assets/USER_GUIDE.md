# User Guide

Welcome to the Holy Bible Mobile App, a rigorous pedagogical tool for studying the AKJV 1611 PCE through the lens of Bible Vector Space Analysis.

## 1. Navigating the 4-Vector Space
The Bible is organized into a mathematical grid using Generalized Coordinates:
- **BOOK (HEIGHT)**: Selection of the 66 Books + Preface.
- **CHAPTER (DEPTH)**: Numerical chapter index.
- **VERSE (LENGTH)**: Numerical verse index.
- **BREADTH (READER)**: The actual word sequence.

Use the Tabs at the top of the Bible section to navigate these dimensions sequentially.

## 2. Choosing a Version (Pedagogy)
Access the **Settings** to choose your level of study:
- **Standard**: Standard AKJV 1611 text.
- **Superscript**: Fixed indexing for every word (Counting One By One).
- **MathKJVP**: Foundation mathematical series (Primary).
- **MathKJVS**: Intermediate mathematical series (Secondary).
- **MathKJVT**: Advanced unconstrained vector analysis (Tertiary).

## 3. Study Hub Tools
- **Limit Table (GRID)**: Observe contextual convergence between "Witnesses" and the term.
- **Dictionary (DICT)**: Perform Inverse Relation searches to see how words are used across the entire Vector Space.
- **Export**: Use the copy icon to capture data formatted for Excel or Lesson Notes.

## 4. Audio & Highlighting
- Toggle audio in Settings. 
- The **Neon Glow** highlight tracks the spoken word across all 5 versions simultaneously.
- Long-press and drag to select contiguous text for sharing to Social Media.

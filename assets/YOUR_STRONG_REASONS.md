The Dictionary uses words as vectors or wood for carpentry of the word of God; and does not rely on human etymology but on the **Use of Words in the Holy Bible**. 
- **The Definition**: expression to be defined is made the limit of all the vectors pointing in its direction in the Bible Vector Space; we need at least 2 or 3 vectors to determine direction.

Using the **Mathematics KJV** logic, the connective "of" is a function word treated as a function operator.
